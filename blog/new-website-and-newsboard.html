📌 Welcome to the News Board
This board is organized into five categories to help you easily track the progress of our project and stay up to date:

  • Announcement
    Important official updates, event notices, and messages from the team.

  • Planned
    Ideas and features currently being prepared. These are items on our roadmap that haven’t started yet.

  • In Progress
    Tasks, stories, or features that are currently being developed or actively happening.

  • Completed
    Items that have been finished and released. You can check here for past events or milestones.

  • Delayed
    Items that were scheduled but have been postponed. New schedules will be shared as soon as they’re ready.

We’ll keep this board regularly updated with the latest news, so be sure to check back often and see what’s coming next! 🐰
